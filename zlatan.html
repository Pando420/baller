The first boss is the "eye of cthulhu". You will need a "suspicius" eye (at night). You can "find suspicius" eyes in underground chests. or you can craft them with 6 lenses dropped by "demon eye" or the "wandering eye". You can also spawn him naturally by having over 180 health (at night).
